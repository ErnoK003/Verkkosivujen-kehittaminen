Who am I?

I am a Finnish guy who has played tens of thousands of hours of video games during his life time. I have played all kinds of games. From strategy games to first person shooters to puzzle games.

Why do I play?

Because I enjoy it. It has connected me with new friends and improved me in many ways. It is also a way to escape real world's stress and pressure. It is also a way to hang out with friends without the restrictions of meeting them and planning ahead. The bar to message friends is very low, when you already see that they are online.
